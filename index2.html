FAQs

Common questions about our adoption process.

Is there a fee to adopt?+
We ask for a small adoption fee to cover vaccines and care. It helps the shelter keep helping more pets. The fee includes initial vaccinations, microchipping, and a starter kit with food and basic supplies.
Do I need parental permission?+
Yes \u2014 kids should get permission from a parent or guardian before applying. We want everyone to be ready for this wonderful responsibility! Parents will need to complete the final paperwork and be present during the home visit.
Can I visit the dog before adopting?+
Yes \u2014 we arrange a meet-and-greet to make sure everyone is comfortable. You can even bring your favorite toy to introduce your new friend! We encourage the whole family to attend this meeting to ensure the dog is a good match for everyone.
What if the adoption doesn't work out?+
We offer a 2-week trial period to ensure the dog is a good fit for your family. If things don't work out, you can return the dog and we'll help find them another loving home. We also provide post-adoption support to help with any adjustment issues.
